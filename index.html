About Us

Engineer or "Engi" as we call it, is the Annual Technical Symposium of NITK Surathkal, to promote technical and managerial enthusiasm amongst young and bright minds of our nation and to provide a platform to transform their innovative ideas into a meaningful reality. You will see the college dressed for the festival in vibrant colors of excitement and joy. It is an amazing experience, we assure you.
