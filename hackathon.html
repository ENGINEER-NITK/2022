The Engineer Hackathon NITK's Annual Technical Fest Hackathon

March 4th-5th 2022

The Engineer Hackathon

The Engineer Hackathon, which is a part of NITK's Annual Technical Fest, is a 24-hour long contest which gives you the opportunity to build something unique to solve existing problems in various spheres of the modern human life. The hackathon

Tracks

Track - 1

Designed to test your daring. Courage in the face of the unknown is an important quality of any coder.

Track - 2

Find your own way to labour against the challenge. Only the most precise, most creative and the fastest will rise to the top.

Track - 3

Oh, attack with vigour, if you can, but be careful. You might just lose your own fort along the way.

Track - 4

Design your way through, but you have to build well and build fast because the clock is always ticking.

Polygon Prizes
Polygon (previously Matic) will be giving away a cash prize of ₹   10,000 for the best hack built on top of Ethereum during the hackathon. For teams that integrate Polygon in their hacks as well, the prize money will be higher, ₹  15000 along with eligibility for internship/full-time role interviews and a chance to land seed funding of upto 5000 USD!
Tezos Foundation Prizes
The Tezos Foundation will be giving away:
  • A cash prize of ₹   20000 for the best Dapp built on Tezos
  • Every valid submission made in the Tezos track will be rewarded with exclusive Schwag Kits
  • Continuity grant opportunity up to 5,000 USD for an outstanding project
Celo Foundation Prizes
The Celo Foundation is giving away ₹   20000 for the best Dapp built on Celo
Protocol Labs Prizes
Protocol Labs is giving away ₹   20000 to a project which makes the best use of IPFS and/or Filecoin
